## Why

The market page was changed into a wide desktop-style table, but the product target is a mobile portrait mini-game demo. The boss also provided a new visual reference with a tavern/card-game style: dark wood panels, gold borders, thick buttons, and a more game-like texture.

## What Changes

- Keep the market page in a mobile portrait layout on desktop and mobile.
- Remove the wide desktop expansion for the market page.
- Restyle the market table with tavern wood, gold trim, dark panels, and game-like buttons.
- Preserve the compact table behavior so one screen can still show many stocks.
- Keep existing 36-stock data, detail navigation, and trading actions working.

## Impact

- Affected files:
  - `styles.css`
  - verification scripts
- No backend/API changes.
- No dependency changes.
